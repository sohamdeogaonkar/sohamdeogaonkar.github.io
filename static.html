3 Ways to Check BSNL Broadband Usage

Posted by Manohar T N. in: How to, Internet,
BSNL (Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited)  is the largest provider of broadband services, fixed telephony in India with more than 60% market share. In that 60% of users, you might be the one who is looking for the information. To know How to get BSNL Broadband usage using SMS and BSNL’s Self-care portal. There are many ways to track your Broadband usage here we will list all the methods. You can choose which way you want as per your convenience. The best and easiest way from my side is Checking usage through SMS. Alternatively, by installing software.


It is better to keep track of your BSNL broadband usage in every way to avoid unnecessary additional amount and it will also ensure you nobody is making unauthorized usage of your broadband account. These are the 3 Ways of checking BSNL Broadband usage.

1. Receiving SMS alerts about usage in Mobile.

This is the easiest way to know the usage of BSNL Broadband. For activating this SMS alerts you just need to send an SMS 

STEP1: First, You need to register your landline number by sending an SMS 
Send SMS as “REG<Space><STD CODE><LANDLINE NUMBER>” to +919448077777 for NON BSNL users. Alternatively, 52295 for BSNL users.
Ex: REG 08X75-31XX84

STEP2: For checking broadband usage, send an SMS “BBU<space><STD CODE><LAND LINE NUMBER>” to +919448077777 for Non BSNL Users or 52295 for BSNL users.
Ex: BBU 08X75-31XX84

2. Installing a software for checking usage.

This is another simple way of tracking the usage; it is actually performed by you if you are not believed in the usage statistics given by BSNL. Here we are going to install a continuous data usage tracking software which will collects how much data you are actually using daily wise, month wise, hour wise and also you can check the speed of your BSNL broadband. There are many software’s out there for tracking data usage we will prefer the one, which is best having less memory and portable.

3. Usage checking through Online in BSNL Self-care Portal.


This is kind of a long process for getting that data usage info but it is perfect compared to the second way. Things you need for this process is your recent BSNL broadband bill because you will get to know some information like Customer ID, Landline number. After all, follow this fully illustrated systematic guide to check your BSNL usage by BSNL portal login.

STEP1: Get to know your BSNL broadband Customer ID/Unique ID and BB user ID



STEP2: Go to the below links as per your regions where you activated your broadband.

Regions(Zones)
BSNL Self-care  URL
South Zone(Andhra Pradesh, Chennai, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamilnadu)
http://selfcare.sdc.BSNL.co.in/
North Zone (Haryana, Punjab, UP(West), UP(East), Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir)
http://selfcare.ndc.BSNL.co.in/
West Zone (Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, Rajasthan )
http://selfcare.wdc.BSNL.co.in/
East Zone (Kolkata, Orissa, Bihar, West Bengal, Andaman Nicobar, Assam, Jharkhand, North East )
http://selfcare.edc.BSNL.co.in/

STEP3: After visiting your respective region BSNL portal click on “User Registration (For Existing BSNL Customers)”


STEP4: Complete the form by providing the relevant information and after completion click next (Most of the fields are mandatory to enter data)


STEP5: In the next step, you will be redirected to agree terms and conditions page. Press agree and then you will see BSNL Portal dashboard.

• Select SERVICES in Dashboard and go to MY SERVICES
• Scroll down a little you will find “Check My Broadband Unbilled Usage Details”
• Select your phone number, Date and range(Current month)
• Then click GO!

STEP6: Now you will see the BSNL broadband usage actually this data used will be shown in MB divide it by 1024 to get values in GB
